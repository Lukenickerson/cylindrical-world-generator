Cylindrical World Generator v0.4 for Procedural Generation Jam 2014
Age: eons
Seed:
  • Press to start/stop aging the world
  • Arrows or WASD to move world
  • ] to raise water, [ to lower water
  • Press space to toggle view
  • +/- to zoom in/out
  • r = raindrop erosion
  • z = smooth
  • x = toggle grid
  • c = coastal erosion
  • v = water volume adjustment
  • m = toggle micro-organisms
  • g = toggle grass
  • p = move plates
  • right-click map = Save as image (in Chrome)
Earlier version 0.3 available on jsfiddle
